About Me

I'm Ernest Okpako — a designer and developer based in Ibadan, Nigeria. I'm passionate about creating digital experiences that are both visually compelling and technically sound.

With a background spanning UI/UX design and full-stack development, I work at the intersection of creativity and technology.

My Story

I started my journey in design, fascinated by the way good visuals can communicate complex ideas instantly. Over time, I found myself increasingly drawn to the engineering side — learning to code so I could bring my own designs to life without compromise.

Today, I work across the full product spectrum: from early concept and wireframing, to polished UI, to production-ready front-end code. I believe the best digital products are built by people who understand both sides of the equation.

I've worked with startups, creative agencies, and independent founders — always with a focus on shipping work that is clean, purposeful, and delightful to use.
